WHO WE ARE

PUMAREJO Y MÁRQUEZ ABOGADOS is a Firm specialized in Civil and Commercial matters that renders comprehensive legal services to both, individuals and legal entities (companies) providing ADVISE AND SOLUTIONS to their legal issues. With over 25 years of experience, we have earned the trust of our customers and derived from the ongoing relationship with them, our Firm has reached solidity with excellence in each of their areas in the professional field.

Hand Tailored

The importance of paying attention to the details is essential for our Firm.

Personalized

We design legal strategies for each particular case, keeping our clients continuously informed through the specialized communication area.

Strategic

Litigation

We provide sponsorship and representation before the Courts, either in the event that our Client is a plaintiff or a defendant, derived from obligations in controversy. The cited intervention falls within the jurisdiction of the Courts in Federal or Local matters in all entities of the Mexican Republic. More

It is noteworthy to mention that the policy in this area is to design procedural strategies to litigate in search of a clear and well-defined objective, discarding any action whose purpose is to litigate aimlessly. Also, we have representation in the United States, in order to protect the interests of our customers even across the border in their business relationship with multinational companies

Corporate Law

In order to verify the corporate status of the company, we review the following instruments:
-The Constitutive Deed.
-The Minute Book.
-The Shareholders' Assembly Acts.
-The Capital Variation Book
-The Shareholder´s registration book.
-The issuance of shares and transmissions of the same.
Done this, we propose a strategic corporate planning previous its regularization. More

We elaborate among others the following documents;

  1. Constitution of Companies with or without foreign investment.
  2. Assembly Minutes that have to do with the:
    • Management report and financial statements.
    • Reduction and Increase of capital.
    • Appointment, ratification or modification of Members of the Board of Directors.
    • Granting or Revoking of Proxies.
    • Mergers or splits.
    • Liquidations

MATTER OF ECONOMIC COMPETITION: In terms of Economic Competition, we take care that the activity of our customers is regulated under our Federal Antitrust Law so that this activity does not fall into monopolies or restrict the efficient functioning of markets for goods and services.
